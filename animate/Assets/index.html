Simulation

In simulation games, players are usually given free control of a character or environment, and there are no clearly defined goals. They can explore real-world scenarios in a safe environment.

Role Play

A role-playing game (RPG) is a game in which each participant assumes the role of a character that can interact within the game's imaginary world. Many RPGs are set in fantasy or science fiction environments.

FPS

FPS stands for "first-person shooter". In these games, players experience the action from the main character's perspective, using handheld weapons to shoot and destroy objects or other players. FPS games are often violent and action-packed.
